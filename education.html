Promotion of Education:
We are committed to ensuring access to free education for all children in Yei. This includes providing financial incentives and salary supplements for teachers, which helps to attract and retain qualified educators in our community. Thanks to these incentives, contributions from families and the community to the schools will be redirected to ensure that each child receives at least one meal per day, helping to address both educational and nutritional needs. Our goal is to improve the quality of education and ensure that no child is left behind due to financial barriers.
