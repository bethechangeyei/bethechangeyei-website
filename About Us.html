ORGANIZATION PROFILE

BE THE CHANGE NZURI DAIMA INICIATIVE (BTC-NDI) is a non political, non profitable and non governmental community base organization granted with a recognition certificate of Operation in Yei river County under chapter 3, section10 0f NGOs Act 2016 in 2024 to Foster sustainable development in the communities of Central Equatoria State through education, environmental stewardship, talent promotion and empowerment of vulnerable women.

Vision:

To build communities where every individual has access to quality education, opportunities for personal and professional growth, and lives in harmony with one another and the environment.

Mission Statement:

BTC-NDI is dedicated to fostering sustainable development in Yei through education, environmental stewardship, talent promotion, and the empowerment of vulnerable women. We strive to create a harmonious and prosperous community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.
